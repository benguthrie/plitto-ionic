Plitto Documentation

Overview

Plitto is a tool that you can use to have great conversations by finding things in common, or interesting things to talk about with your existing friends, and new friends.

Conversations are made of listening, and celebrating what we share. Plitto helps you make conversations better by highligting things that matter to you.

Strangers & Friends

Friend icon:

A friend is someone who you've added as a connection in Facebook.

Future: Plitto will have it's own set of connections.

Stranger icon:

A stranger is a real person, but you don't know who they are. You can still comment and ditto their things as an easy way to build up your content.

There will be a way to meet them in the future.
