Plitto Documentation

Overview

Plitto is a tool that you can use to have great conversations by finding things in common, or interesting things to talk about with your existing friends, and new friends.

Conversations are made of listening, and celebrating what we share. Plitto helps you make conversations better by highligting things that matter to you.

Ditto

A "ditto" is a way of saying "me too". Use this to connect with people in ways that you want to connect. Be as serious or playful as you want. Nobody knows how to use Plitto yet, so go ahead and do whatever you want. You activity is only public to you, and the person you're dittoing from.

This icon means you share this item within this context. Press to remove this item.

Lists

Lists hold things and provide context for things. You may have an item in common with someone, but they may have it in the "I Love" list, and you may have it in the "Deal Breakers" list. The list context is valuable.

This is the list icon.
Press this to add a list, or find a list to add to.
Strangers & Friends

Plitto lets you interact with friends and Strangers. You can only see the identities of the people who you already know, but you can ditto things from strangers.

This is the icon for friends. Generally, we try to show you an actual picture, and not this generic icon.
