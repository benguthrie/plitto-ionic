Security & Privacy
Plitto will make money off of the connections it makes outside of your friend base. That will allow Plitto to thrive without money from advertisers or researchers, which will allow you to feel more secure adding your content.
Who can see my content?

Currently, all your Facebook friends could see anything you put into Plitto. This will evolve over time.

Strangers can also see your data, but they can't see that it's associated with you. Your data will be aggregated among other strangers, and all of your identities will be removed when it is viewed.

So don't put in content like "The best things about being Jennifer Jones, SSN 111-12-3123 / Living on 123 Elm Street / Being Born Jan 1, 1990 / Making $34,000 per year". Unless you're up for some excitement to see just how bad identity theft can be.
